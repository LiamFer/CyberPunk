Runners

Cyberpunk Edgerunners is a highly anticipated new series set in the iconic Night City, a neon-soaked dystopian metropolis filled with danger, intrigue, and advanced technology. In this world, people have to fight tooth and nail to survive, and the Edgerunners are the most skilled and fearless of them all.

Lucy is a fierce and fearless Edgerunner, with a talent for close combat and a love for high-risk jobs. With her razor-sharp reflexes and her relentless determination

David Martinez is one of the most skilled Edgerunners in Night City. With his sharpshooting abilities and tactical mind

Rebecca is a rising star in the Edgerunner world, known for her exceptional hacking skills and quick thinking.

Sample Text

As an Edgerunner, you are a rebel, a mercenary, a hacker, and an outlaw all at once. You take on the toughest jobs, from corporate espionage to sabotage, assassination, and theft. You are the ultimate survivor, using your wits and your cybernetic enhancements to stay one step ahead of the competition and the law.
